What is it?

Human impact is how humans interact with the ecosystem It can be for the better, or it can cuase distruction. In an ecosystem, the balance of the ecosystem is called ecolibrium. When ecolibrium has been reached, an ecosystem in considered equal, or sustainable.

Human impact can affect the balance of a ecosystem.

Human impact affects the plants, animals, and the climate in an ecosystem. This may include changing the tempature and humidity of an enviornment.

Ok, now what actually cuases it? Pollution is what leads to most of the negitave human impact. There are two kinds of pollution, point source, and non point scource.

Yes! Human impcat is completly preventable! Here's what you can do to help:

3. Clean! Clean! Clean! - If you go to a near by creek or stream, you'll likley find lots of trash. You know what to do!

What do we do now?

Help the enviornment. We need to focus on cleanup. We need to rebuild the enviornment. That could include filtering water, cleaning up trash in the water, and testing backup systems for future use.
